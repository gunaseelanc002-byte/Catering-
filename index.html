About Us

Authentic South Indian
Catering Since Years

Sri Angalamman Catering is a trusted name in Tamil Nadu for delivering authentic South Indian food for all types of functions. Based in Erode, we proudly serve across Tirupur, Coimbatore, Salem, Dharmapuri and Namakkal.

From traditional breakfast spreads to grand wedding feasts, our expert team ensures every meal is prepared fresh and served with warmth and professionalism.
